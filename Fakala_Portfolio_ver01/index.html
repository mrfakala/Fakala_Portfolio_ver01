FAKALA AS A MUSICIAN

In 2022, made his debut on digital music platforms with his work "Denge". This work strongly demonstrates Fakala's storytelling side. In 2022, he released an album called "Enteresantel" which conveyed his music learning process and the confusing problems of a teenager. Released 2 singles titled "Yanımda Ol" and "AMA BEN SİKTİR ETMEK İSTEMİYORUMM!!".

In 2023, released a single "Hayat Boktan". In 2024, released two singles "Tinnitus" and "Gözlerini Kapat ve Sus". "Tinnitus" was listened to more than 20,000 times on Spotify within 2 months. On May 31, 2024, "En Güzel İlhamlar", the first piece from his album "Karanlık Sancıyı Aydınlatıyor", was pre released. The track entered the Spotify "Fresh Finds Türkiye" list at number one. The track from this album "Teşhis" has reached more than 40,000 listens on Spotify.

Performed as Fakala. Some of them has videos on Youtube. After the Album "Karanlık Sancıyı Aydınlatıyor" has released, 4 different internet magazines (Rotka, BBI, Müzik Ekspres, Beatsommelier) has published an article about “Fakala”. The song “En Güzel İlhamlar” from this album has stayed on the first place of “Fresh Finds Türkiye” editorial list for a month. Directed and published 3 music videos as “Fakala”.
